This is my happy little webpage where I indulge in my interests, and is a way to organize my life on the web.

I will talk about my D&D campaign, my projects, my schoolwork, etc. It is also just a way to keep what I like close without having 1000 bookmarks.

This site is mainly for me, but hey, look as you please.
